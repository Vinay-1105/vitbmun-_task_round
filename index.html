About Us

Welcome to the VITB Model United Nations! Our mission is to empower students with diplomacy, leadership, and public speaking skills through the simulation of UN conferences. Through debates on global issues, participants develop critical thinking, negotiation, and teamwork. VITB MUN is cultural awareness and preparation for the next generation of leaders, confident and creative in handling real-world challenges.
